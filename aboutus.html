MEET THE TRAINERS

DANIEL OH

Born and bred in Koreatown Los Angeles, Danny has always been active. In high school he competed in varsity football, ran the 300m and 110m hurdles in track & field, volleyball, and martial arts. Danny's expertise in sports performance and strength conditioning has helped athletes improve their speed, power, agility and overall strength. Since 2009, Danny has trained professional athletes, celebrities, youths and everyday people who are wanting to get in shape and perform better. Certifications: • NASM (National Academy of Sports Medicine) Certified Personal Trainer • NASM Performance Enhancement Specialist • Lifeguard • Black Belt in Tae Kwon Do Language: English, Korean

JOHNNY SUH

Born in South Korea but raised most of his life in New Jersey, Johnny didn't realize his natural athleticism until he got involved with varsity football and wrestling in high school where he was captained in the respective sports. Johnny specializes in strength conditioning, mma workout, and competes in natural body building. Certifications: • NASM Certified Personal Trainer • NASM Fitness Nutrition Specialist • Lifeguard • Black Belt in Tae Kwon Do Languages: English, Korean

OMAR TRANCOSO

Raised in San Fernando Valley, Omar has always been involved with sports and power lifting. He pursued his degree in Kinesiology from Cal State University, Northridge, for physical therapy, but then switched to Exercise Science upon discovery of his true passion: fitness and performance enhancement. After college, Omar learned valuable skills working as an assistant strength and conditioning coach at Pierce College for the Men's sports team and also at a rehab clinic. Omar specializes in sports performance, functional exercise, and powerlifting. Certifications: • B.S. Kinesiology, w/ Exercise Science Specialization • NCSA: Certified Strength & Conditioning Specialist • ACSM: Certified Personal Trainer

RONAIL "STRETCH" SHELTON

Ronail is a former collegiate athlete in Track and Field's 400m hurdles and majored in Kinesiology. For the past 6 years, Ronail worked as a personal trainer for Gold's Gym, and also ran bootcamps for his clients. He is currently a high school coach for track and cross country while also training clients full time. Accomplishments: • Northern California and California State Champion in the 400 intermediate hurdles. • NASM Certified Personal Trainer

MAYAMI OYANAGI

Mayami received her Masters degree in Physical Therapy from University of Hartford 2007. Her interest in the physical therapy began based on her own low back injury experience at a young age. Her clinical experience spans 8 years, concentrating on orthopedic manual therapy and sports medicine. She treats the “root-cause” of a client’s problem by utilizing biomechanical assessments. She continues to take various continuing education courses all over the nation to improve her skills and help people get better and move better faster. She enjoys hiking, swimming, skiing, and motorcycle racing. Accomplishments: • Orthopedic Physical Therapy
